# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection
We collect the following data to provide our services:
- Account information (email, name)
- Interview recordings (video, audio)
- Usage analytics
- Device information

## Data Processing
- Interview recordings are processed for AI analysis
- Data is encrypted at rest and in transit
- Processing occurs in secure data centers

## Data Retention
- Interview recordings: 30 days after deletion request
- Account data: Until account deletion
- Analytics: Anonymized after 1 year

## User Rights
- Access your data
- Download your data
- Request deletion
- Opt out of analytics
